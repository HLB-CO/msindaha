Mousa Sindaha

Managing Partner

Mousa is the oldest son of late Michel Sindaha, the founder of the firm. After graduating from California State University Beach in 1982, he joined his father as a junior accountant. He qualified as JCPA in 1984 and was promoted to partnership. While working and serving in the army, Mousa also earned a Law degree from Beirut Arab University in 1985.

In 1992, Mousa took the CPA exam from the State of Montana, USA. He holds licenses to practice in Montana as well as Jordan. Mousa has focused on training and developing firm employees and served as Chairman of the Training Committee of the Jordan Association of CPA’s (JACPA). From 2003 to 2006, he was a member of JACPA’s board, and from 2011 to 2014, he served as its Treasurer. Since January 2014, Mousa has been a member of the Compliance Advisory Panel (CAP) of the International Federation of Accountants (IFAC), with his term ending in 2019.

Beyond his professional career, Mousa has been active in community service. He is a past president of the Rotary Club of Amman Jerash and has delivered several articles and speeches on Rotary history in Jordan, Palestine, and the Middle East.

Mousa also has a deep interest in history, having written several research papers on the subject.
