Saleh Al-Zaher

Operations Manager

Saleh is responsible for overseeing payroll operations across multiple locations within a region.

His core responsibilities include:

  1. Ensure efficient, accurate, and compliant payroll processing for clients.
  2. Manage and strengthen client relationships to maintain satisfaction and retention.
  3. Supervise and guide regional payroll teams to achieve performance goals.
  4. Implement and monitor process improvements for operational efficiency.
  5. Ensure adherence to local labor laws and regulatory requirements.
  6. Collaborate with internal teams to support business development and client onboarding.
  7. Analyze and resolve payroll-related issues promptly.
  8. Monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) to ensure service quality.

As a Payroll Operations Manager, Saleh is responsible for driving operational excellence in payroll services, fostering client satisfaction, and ensuring compliance with local regulations while managing regional teams and optimizing processes.
